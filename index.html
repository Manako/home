Production Work

Storyloom

Storyloom was a UGC platform for creating and sharing stories available on iOS and Android and web. My team was responsible for the front-end development on web, including the discovery and FTUE. Additionally I worked with an external team to develop some of the internal CMS tools.

Old World

I was the sole producer credited on this project, which won 2021 PC Gamer Best Strategy Game. I was responsible for the overall production of the game, including the schedule and team management of 50 FTEs and contractors. I worked closely with the contract art team to ensure their assets were delievered to a high quality standard and prioritized their backlog.

Barbie Malibu Mysteries.

One of a number of games I produced while at 1st Playable Productions, this game was made in partnership with Leapfrog and featured the Barbie brand. I was responosible for the overall design and production of the game, including the schedule and scope and insured a timely delivery of quality features to our client.

About Me

When I was young my father taught me I could do anything, and I may have taken it to mean I could do everything. I come from a programming background and did game design in my early career and am now an experienced game producer. My favorite game genres are strategy and simulation, but I find joy in all the games where I have made contributions. I am currently a producer at Epic Games and live in upstate New York with my family and two cats, someday I dream of owning sheep.

Production Philosophy

Strong teams build great games. My production philosophy is to work with teams to develop a process that will best achieve their short and long term goals. I develop rituals that allow for feedback and continuous improvement of our processes while also capturing data to predict potential risk to our schedules.

I have production experience on small and medium sized teams with budgets ranging from 10k to 10 million+. I have worked on licensed commercial games for entertainment and education, unique IP and have experience working on PC, Mobile and console. Listen to my podcast from when I worked on Old World.
